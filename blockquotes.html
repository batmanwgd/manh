Blockquotes


Blockquotes indicate that the enclosed text is an extended quotation. Usually, this is rendered visually by indentation, as seen below. A URL for the source of the quote may be given using the cite attribute, while a simple text representaion of the source can be given by using the HTML < cite > element.



Example

“There are three responses to a piece of design – yes, no, and WOW! Wow is the one to aim for.”
- Paul Rand

Code

copy 
<blockquote>
    “There are three responses to a piece of design – yes, no, and WOW! Wow is the one to aim for.”
<blockquote>
<cite>- Paul Rand<cite>
