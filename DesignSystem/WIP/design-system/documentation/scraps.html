atoms

The basic building blocks of our entire design system. Each element has distinct properties, and they can't be broken down further without loosing their meaning.

molecules

Groups of two or more atoms held together by their associated bonds. These combinations of atoms take on their own unique properties, and become more tangible and operational than atoms

organisms

Assemblies of molecules functioning together as one cohesive unit. These relatively complex systems can range from single organisms to incredibly sophistocated.

pages

Standards for specific instances of templates that showcase a particular UI standard with real or placeholder content. Pages also provide a place to articulate variations in templates.
